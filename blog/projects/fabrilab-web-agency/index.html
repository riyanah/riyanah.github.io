Creation of a web design agency.

In 2017, I created with a partner a communication agency specialized in digital. We collaborate with small and large companies in their digital transformation. As a creative agency, we have developed ideas, products and digital experiences to better connect our clients with their customers.

Fabrilab's purpose:

  • 💾 Making digital experiences accessible
  • 🌿 Host websites on shared and more environmentally friendly servers
  • 🌐 Expert in digital strategy to increase visibility on the web
  • 🚀 Training and support our clients throughout the project

Achievements:

Since the creation of our company, we have accompanied about 20 companies in the digital. This type of project has allowed us to establish ourselves as experts on digital subjects:

  • Development & creation of an e-commerce website for Toy Surfboards;
  • Various graphic design projects for TotalEnergies.

Solutions for every need:

  • Website creation & deployment
  • Graphic design
  • Digital strategy consulting
  • Analysis & improvement
